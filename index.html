Understanding Key Disorders

Depression (MDD)

Persistent low mood and loss of interest in activities for a long period.

Symptoms: Extreme sadness, fatigue, sleep changes.
Identity Disorder (DID)

Presence of multiple, distinct personality states that control behavior.

Symptoms: Memory gaps, fragmented identity.
Social Anxiety (SAD)

Intense, persistent fear of being watched and judged by others in social settings.

Symptoms: Trembling, avoiding social interactions.
Maladaptive Daydreaming

Immersive fantasy activity that replaces regular social and human interaction.

Symptoms: Difficulty focusing, vivid fantasy life.
PTSD

Symptoms following a traumatic event, including flashbacks and anxiety.

Symptoms: Nightmares, hypervigilance.
General Anxiety (GAD)

Constant and excessive worry about everyday life, often without any direct cause.

Symptoms: Restlessness, irritability, muscle tension.
